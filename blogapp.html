by Leah Wan

What is Sphere?

Sphere is a personal concept I created inpired by Instagram. The inspiration came from my habit of following fashion bloggers on their own sites, where they put up links to the clothing from their photos with a small story about the outfit. I found it would be super useful if there is a mobile blogger app that could share those shoppable links in each post, and encourage direct purchase within the same app.

Design Process

Identify User Needs & Pain Points

By running interviews and surveys with target users, we learned more about their behavior and needs under specific scenarios, as well as daily habits and shopping patterns. These data helped us identify the major pain points and the goal of this product.

Comparative Analysis

One of the key steps before ideation is comparative analysis. It helps us understand better about the currant market and major players.

Ideation & Initial Design

We started with grouping our users into three major categories: browser/starter, blogger, expert. The initial design is focused on creating the user flow for browser/starter with simple and straightforward features.

Iterate & Test

The next step is running user test with the initial designs and collect feedback, which would help us iterate and finalize the design.
